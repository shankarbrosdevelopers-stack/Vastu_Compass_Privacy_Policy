Offline Data & Privacy

Vastu Compass is designed to be a primarily offline application. We respect your privacy and aim to minimize data collection.

The App includes an AI-powered chat powered by the Google Gemini API. This feature requires an active internet connection.

  • Data Transmission: When you interact with the AI chat, your text prompts are transmitted to Google's servers to generate a response.

  • Privacy Advice: Please avoid sharing any sensitive or personal information (such as financial details or private identification) within the AI chat interface.
